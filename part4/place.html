Minas Tirith

Host: Aragorn, son of Arathorn

Description: 7 floors, no elevator, but a breathtaking view of the battles! Perfect for lovers of white stone, dramatic balconies, and orc neighbors that get a little too close.

Amenities

  • Panoramic anti-orc balcony
  • 100% natural cardio stairs
  • Emergency bell (crisis included)
  • Wi-Fi launched straight from Osgiliath
  • Nazgûl-proof certified stone wall

Price by night : 120€

Helm’s Deep

Host: Éowyn

Description: The perfect getaway if you like cold stone, tight spaces, and the thrill of imminent invasion. Thick walls, natural air conditioning, and complimentary catapult service. Ideal for cozy nights with 10,000 screaming Uruk-hai. Bonus: spacious cave in case of emergency exit.

Amenities

  • Vaulted halls with almost soundproofing
  • Elf launch ramp (limited use)
  • Optional troll cistern
  • Built-in rainstorm during attacks
  • Emergency cave-based exit system

Price by night : 50€

Foncombe

Host: Elrond

Description: An upscale Elvish retreat, perfect for lovers of cryptic poetry, 24/7 harp concerts, and slow-motion stair walks. The ideal place to heal your wounds… or avoid your problems. Warning: high risk of philosophical monologues.

Amenities

  • Zen fountain with existential reflections
  • Self-service harpists
  • Elven library (endless scrolls included)
  • Natural light filtered by ancient wisdom
  • Beds that recite Lórien poetry at night

Price by night : 150€

Mordor

Host: Sauron

Description: A volcanic hotspot for fans of extreme heat, barren landscapes, and being constantly watched by a giant flaming eye. No sunlight, guaranteed toxic vibes. Perfect for introverts… and megalomaniac overlords.

Amenities

  • Direct view of Mount Doom (heating included)
  • 24/7 giant flaming eye surveillance
  • Grass-free guaranteed lava floors
  • Unfiltered sulfur-rich air
  • Express mail service via Nazgûl

Price by night : 10€

Mines of Moria

Host: Gimli, son of Glóin

Description: Once a luxury mine, now a full-scale escape room experience. Ideal for echo lovers, creepy corridors, and things you really shouldn’t wake up. Comes with dust, darkness, and complimentary drums of doom.

Amenities

  • 10 km² grand entrance hall
  • Pet Balrog (not house-trained)
  • Deep drums in surround sound™
  • Broken bridge (members only)
  • Random staircases (no exit guaranteed)

Price by night : 70€

The Prancing Pony

Host: The Butterpear Family

Description: Rustic charm in the heart of Bree. Warm ales, loud bards, and paper-thin walls. Great for laying low while on the run from Ringwraiths (black cloak not included).

Amenities

  • Unlimited semi-cold ale
  • Hobbit songs at sunset
  • Authentic squeaky wooden locks
  • Guests: 50% mysterious, 50% drunk
  • Unattended pony parking out back

Price by night : 80€
